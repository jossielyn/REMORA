How REMORA Works:
The main source of electricity that powers the REMORA is hydropower. Using the power of the flow of water, we can capture kinetic energy. This movement of energy can be used to move other objects, like a turbine or generator which can convert the energy and power of the flowing water into electricity Hydropower is a good alternative since itâ€™s sustainable through earths natural water cycle and one of the most cost effective sources of renewable energy.The water power generator is directly connected to the suction motor through a water tight connection. The suction motor, which creates the vacuum pressure and suction by rotating a motor fan, is located in a waterproof, sealed compartment in the bottom part of the REMORA. Suction motor creates an airflow that then travels through a hose, which wraps around the outer shell and allows for the suction effect by which waste within a 10 to 15ft radius will begin to slowly fall into the REMORA. The vacuum effect takes place in intervals as to not put the lives of marine lives in danger. Within the REMORA, there are two shells, the outer shell, and the inner shell, which is the one that collects the waste. The net is made of a nylon material and is lined by oil absorbent pads. The pads work by trapping any liquid pollutants in the water and leaving behind just water due to its hydrophobic properties. The water exits the inner shell though a small pipe-like opening that is lined with a micro mesh which allows for an extra filtration of the water. The water then completely exits the REMORA through another pipes-like opening located on the bottom of the outer shell. This pipe has two doors, one inside, where the water is, and one outside. These doors are controlled by a water level sensor in order to avoid an overfilling of water within the REMORA structure. The top of the innershtell has a metal handle that allows it to be easily removed and replaced from the REMORA without having remove the whole structure from the water Additionally, the top of the structure has two metal doors that allow it to close in case of inclement weather to prevent damage to the REMORA. To simplify the process of maintenance, our app allows checking the status of the REMORA in case of damage or if a replacement is needed in case the REMORA is full.
